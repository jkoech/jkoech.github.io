Logistic services

NKN Transporter has a particular focus on servicing the oil and gas sector in East Africa, which offers the following services;

  • Loading of consolidation services
  • Receiving and dispatching of goods
  • Clearing and forwarding of goods
  • Safe and secure vehicle parking
  • Local collections and deliveries to and from warehouses, retail stations for load consolidation
  • Insurance of goods to the value of declared invoices
  • Dealing with transporting hazardous goods

Service delivery

NKN Transporters is comprehensively equipped with a wide range of well maintained transportation equipment and vehicles. To ensure a high quality, efficient and prompt service, the average age of our fleet is maintained at three years and new vehicles are purchased every two years.

Our vehicles are regularly serviced and strictly maintained to set manufacturer maintenance schedules. A high level of drivertraining maintains the excellent standard of our fleet.

Domestic Transportation

Our Business: we maintain our local focus in the Western and Northern part of Kenya. In Kenya, we currently provide logistics services for:

  • Transportation of Petroleum and Fuel Products
  • Transportation of bulk
  • Dry goods

In handling transportation of the above goods, the company has separate vehicles and trained staff that are in compliant with regulations from the following regulators;

  • National Environmental and Management Authority
  • Ministry of Health guidelines for transporting
  • Kenya Revenue Authority
  • National Transport and Safety Authority
  • Energy Regulatory Commission
  • The company is also compliant with the standardized practice for transporting highly flammable goods - IG-55.1.2

International Transportation

With several trucks dedicated to cross-border transportation, International Transportation currently consist of petroleum products and dry goods products. NKN has separate vehicles that are in compliant with the regulations required by Customs & Border Control for the East African Community Market.
